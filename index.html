MARK CABUNOC

Pop / Adult Contemporary / Nerd Rock
 

Mark Cabunoc is a singer-songwriter and composer in San Francisco, California via Toronto, Canada. After studying Classical and Jazz music various instruments throughout his life, Mark decided in 2011 to pursue a career as a singer-songwriter thanks to influences such as Jason Mraz, Sara Bareilles and Andy Grammer. It was soon after this decision that Mark wrote his first song and found that he had a natural flair for it. Mark moved to San Francisco in 2014.
